How To Play:

Put the same animals together to make solid chain that consists of 3 or more animals. The last place where you put the animal composing the chain, will become next level animal. The bigger is animal's level, the more score you'll get for it. Also you get score for putting animals on the field. The square on the left from the field shows the animal you will put now.
